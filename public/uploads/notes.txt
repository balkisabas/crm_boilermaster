step to install/run project crm

1. composer install
2. composer require spatie/laravel-permission
3. composer require laravelcollective/html
4. php artisan vendor:publish --provider="Spatie\Permission\PermissionServiceProvider"
5. php artisan migrate
6. php artisan db:seed --class=PermissionTableSeeder
7. php artisan db:seed --class=CreateAdminUserSeeder
